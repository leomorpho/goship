## Description
Objective: Add an end-to-end suite that generates a fresh app, migrates it, boots it, and verifies it.

Context:
This is the confidence loop for fresh apps. The framework should prove that a brand-new app can boot, migrate, render, and verify without handholding.

- Prefer one obvious convention, delete fallback paths, and keep the generated or framework-owned surface beautiful enough to feel like Rails or Laravel.
